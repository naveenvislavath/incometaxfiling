Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our income tax filing services.

What documents do I need to provide for income tax filing?

For salaried individuals, you'll need to provide Form 16 from your employer, Form 26AS (tax credit statement), investment proofs for tax-saving deductions (80C, 80D, etc.), home loan interest certificate (if applicable), and rent receipts (if claiming HRA). For business owners and professionals, additional documents like financial statements, bank statements, and expense receipts will be required.

How long does it take to complete my tax filing?

Once you've provided all the necessary documents, we typically complete the tax filing process within 3-5 business days. For more complex cases involving business income or capital gains, it may take 5-7 business days. We always prioritize accuracy over speed to ensure you get the maximum tax benefits and remain fully compliant.

What if I receive a tax notice after filing?

If you receive a tax notice after filing, don't worry. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include year-round notice support. Simply forward the notice to us, and our expert team will analyze it, prepare the appropriate response, and guide you through the resolution process. For Basic plan users, notice support is available at an additional fee.

Can you help with tax planning throughout the year?

Yes, we offer year-round tax planning services to help you make informed financial decisions that optimize your tax liability. Our tax experts can provide guidance on investment strategies, expense management, and other tax-saving opportunities specific to your financial situation. This service is included in our Enterprise plan and available as an add-on for other plans.

How secure is my financial information with you?

We take data security extremely seriously. All your financial information is protected with bank-level 256-bit encryption. Our secure portal uses SSL technology for document uploads, and we have strict internal access controls. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. Our systems comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

What if I need to file revised returns?

If you discover additional information or errors after your return has been filed, we can help you file a revised return. For Professional and Enterprise plan users, one revised return filing is included at no extra cost. Basic plan users can avail this service for an additional fee. Revised returns can be filed within the time limit prescribed by the Income Tax Department.
